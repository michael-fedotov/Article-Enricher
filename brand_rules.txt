Brand Voice
-----------
• Friendly‑expert tone: informative but approachable.  
• Active voice, concise sentences (average 18–20 words).  
• Inclusive language; avoid jargon unless defined.

Accessibility & Alt‑Text
------------------------
• Every image must have descriptive alt‑text ≤ 125 characters.  
• Do **not** begin alt‑text with "Image of" or "Picture of".  
• Decorative images should use empty alt attributes (`alt=""`).  
• Ensure heading hierarchy (H1→H2→H3) and semantic Markdown.  
• Anchor text must be descriptive—never "click here".

Style Guidelines
----------------
• Use sentence‑case for headings.  
• Numbers zero through nine spelled out; 10 and above as numerals.  
• Limit asterisks and italics to emphasis; avoid ALL‑CAPS.
• Do not use em dashes or common AI sounding text

Legal & Attribution
-------------------
• Only use assets from `media.db` and `links.db`.  
• Do not alter asset URLs.  
• Cite statistics as footnotes when possible
